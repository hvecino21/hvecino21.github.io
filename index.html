About me

Consultant and ICT Expert - With over two decades of experience in IT, cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, and business architecture, I have had the opportunity to work across various sectors including finance, telecommunications, and public services. Throughout my career, I have developed a unique ability to integrate and implement technology solutions from different vendors, ensuring seamless and efficient operations while maintaining a strong focus on security and compliance.

What are my professional goals?

As a solutions architect, I focus on designing and managing cloud architectures that are scalable, secure, and efficient, always aiming to optimize performance and reduce costs. My objective is to ensure that the solutions I implement align with business needs, allowing them to grow flexibly and sustainably. I prioritize security at every layer of the architecture, applying proper access controls and safeguarding data integrity.

Additionally, I continuously strive to optimize resources, ensuring they are used strategically and efficiently to avoid waste and unnecessary expenses. My goal is also to promote process automation, improving the speed and efficiency of development and deployment. Through close collaboration with business teams, I translate their objectives into practical technological solutions, ensuring a balanced approach between innovation, security, and cost.
